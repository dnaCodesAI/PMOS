# Opportunity Solution Tree

## Step 1 — Desired outcome
State the outcome we're trying to produce.

## Step 2 — Opportunity identification
Generate 5 opportunity areas. For each: name, one-sentence user pain,
Impact (H/M/L), Confidence (H/M/L).

## Step 3 — Solution directions
For the top opportunity, generate 3 different solution directions with
trade-offs and the riskiest assumption.

## Step 4 — Experiment recommendation
Recommend one solution to test first, with rationale.
