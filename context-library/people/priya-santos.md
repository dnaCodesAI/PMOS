# Priya Santos — Head of Growth & GTM Lead

## Role
Head of Growth Marketing at Lakeview Bank — owns the launch plan, in-app
messaging, push notification copy, and the onboarding re-engagement campaign tied
to this feature. Also owns opt-in rate for goal milestone notifications as a
shared KPI with PM.

## Communication style
Collaborative and deadline-driven. Thinks in campaigns and user segments, not
feature specs. Wants to be involved before the feature is built, not handed a
spec to market after engineering is done. Responds best to a shared doc with a
clear owner column — she will fill her lane if the lane is clearly drawn. Follows
up persistently when she doesn't have what she needs; prefers a rough draft on
time over a polished brief late.

## What she cares about most
- Push notification opt-in rate — she owns this number and needs the PM to
  confirm the notification trigger logic before she writes copy against it
- The empty state and newly-onboarded user experience — her re-engagement campaign
  depends on users having at least one goal to return to; a dead feature surface
  kills her funnel before it starts
- Launch timing aligned to a campaign window — she has a Q3 mobile savings
  campaign already in market planning; a slip past week 8 forces her to reschedule
  paid media
