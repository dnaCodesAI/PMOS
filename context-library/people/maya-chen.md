# Maya Chen — Engineering Lead

## Role
Senior Engineering Lead, Mobile Platform — owns the technical architecture for
the Savings Goals feature and the notification infrastructure decision (build vs.
integrate). Reports to the VP of Engineering. Two engineers on her team for this
sprint.

## Communication style
Async-first. Prefers written specs over verbal walkthroughs. Reads the acceptance
criteria before the overview. Will not commit to a timeline until open questions
have written answers. Surfaces blockers early and expects the same from PM. Does
not respond well to scope changes after sprint planning — bring changes before
the sprint starts or expect pushback.

## What she cares about most
- Whether the data model supports the feature without a migration (open question 1
  is her top concern — she needs an answer before estimation)
- Clear, testable acceptance criteria she can hand directly to engineers without
  rewriting them
- Not being asked to build notification infrastructure mid-sprint — she needs the
  build-vs-integrate decision made at kickoff, not week four
