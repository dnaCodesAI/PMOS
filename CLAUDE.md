# Role
I am the lead Product Manager at Lakeview Bank. My product is the Savings Goals
feature — a mobile experience that lets banking customers set, track, and achieve
savings goals without leaving the app.

# Product Context
- Stage: MVP — core goal-tracking loop being built for first ship
- North Star Metric: % of users who return to their goal view within 30 days of
  onboarding (current: 20%, target: 80%)
- Business model: Retention — the bank loses 23% of mobile users by day 90;
  reducing that churn directly protects deposit volume and lifetime customer value

# Target Users
1. Active mobile saver (avg. age 34, 1–3 goals set at onboarding) — sets goals
   but never returns because the app gives no feedback on whether they're on track
2. Newly onboarded user with no goals yet — lands on a dead feature surface with
   no prompt to engage; needs a clear entry point before they disengage entirely

# My Stakeholders
- Engineering Lead — owns data model and notification infrastructure — prefers
  specificity over vision; cares about migration complexity for existing users'
  goals and whether iOS/Android notification service needs to be built or integrated
- Designer — owns the goal detail screen and progress indicators — collaborative,
  responds well to user story framing; cares that progress legibility doesn't
  require users to do mental arithmetic
- Banking/Compliance — reviews anything customer-facing in a regulated context —
  risk-averse, document-first; cares that edge cases (stale data, failed loads,
  goal deletion) are explicitly handled and never silently fail
